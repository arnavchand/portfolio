Daily Logo Challenge

Jan-Feb'20

30 days of Creativity!

Daily Logo Challenge is an online graphic design challenge, in which a logo brief is sent through email everyday. I decided to take up the challenge for an opportunity to be creative everyday and to hone my logo building skills. On an average I devoted 1.5 hours on a single logo.

Project Type:

Personal Project

My Role:

Graphic Designer

Services:

Graphic Design, Logo Design

Overview

Being from India, I have tried to incorporate the festivals, traditions, weathers of India (occurring in that particular month) in the corresponding artwork.
