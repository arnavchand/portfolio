A Calendar without Colors!

This project contains artwork made for a calendar. I wanted to make something that stood out among the typical & monotonous calendars that we usually come across. I chose the grayscale theme as a challenge to capture the essence of each month without using any colours.

Project Type:

Personal Project

My Role:

Graphic Designer

Services:

Graphic Design, Poster Design

Overview

Being from India, I have tried to incorporate the festivals, traditions, weathers of India (occurring in that particular month) in the corresponding artwork.
